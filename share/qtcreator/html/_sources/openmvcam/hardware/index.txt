Datasheets for the components on the OpenMV Cam
===============================================

* The microcontroller: `STM32F427VG <http://www.st.com/en/microcontrollers/stm32f427vg.html>`_
* The camera: `Omnivision OV7725 <http://www.haoyuelectronics.com/Attachment/OV7725%20+%20AL422B(FIFO)%20Camera%20Module(V1.0)/OV7725%20datasheet(V1.2).pdf>`_
* The LDO voltage regulator: `ROHM BD33IA <http://pdf1.alldatasheet.com/datasheet-pdf/view/532036/ROHM/BD33IA5WEFJ.html>`_

Datasheets for other components

* The LCD display on the LCD shield: `Sitronix ST7735R <https://cdn-shop.adafruit.com/datasheets/ST7735R_V0.2.pdf>`_

* The BLE Soc on the BLE shield: `ACKme AMS002 <https://zentri.ack.me/products/AMS002_Bobcat_LC>`_
